## Where the built parts live

| Piece | File |
|-------|------|
| The aggregations | `src/features/history/historyStats.ts`, `computeHistoryStats` |

`computeHistoryStats` is the canonical definition of "this week", "reroll rate"
and "most active time". It returns `weekCount`, `allTime`, `rerollRate`,
`mostActive`, `moduleCounts` and `weekBars`.

## What is still open

The aggregation runs on the phone. Every load walks the whole decision list and
recomputes it. That is fine at thirty rows, not at three thousand, and it cannot
see decisions made on another device. Decisions already mirror to Firestore, but
nothing aggregates them there.

| Work | Done when |
|------|-----------|
| Weekly aggregation over the mirrored decisions in Firestore | Queries return the correct counts |
| Same field names as `computeHistoryStats` | One definition of each figure, not two |

If the field names match, swapping the screen over to read the summary is close
to a one-line change.

Data shape, sources and the helpers to reuse:
`briefs/sprint-3/gamification-data-shape-for-dashboard.md`.
